Complex Contrast Training (CCT) Workout

Main Complex Sets (3-4 sets each, 3-5 min rest between sets)

Session Summary

  • Type: Power
  • Focus: Full-body explosive power
  • Sets: 3–4 per block
  • Rest: 3–5 min per block
  • Frequency: 2x/week (most), up to 3x/week (elite). Max 3x/week due to CNS demand.

1. Chest/Push Focus

A1: Barbell Bench Press - 3-5 reps @ 85-90% 1RM

A2: Plyometric Push-Ups or Clap Push-Ups - 5-8 reps (focus on speed/explosiveness)

2. Shoulders/Vertical Push

B1: Push Press or Dumbbell Arnold Press - 3-5 reps

B2: Overhead Medicine Ball Slams or Shot Put Throws - 6-8 reps

3. Back/Pull Focus

C1: Weighted Pull-Ups or Barbell Rows - 4-6 reps

C2: Band-Resisted Row Explosions or Explosive Renegade Rows - 6-8 reps

4. Rotational Power

D1: Landmine Twist (Heavy) - 6 reps per side

D2: Rotational Medicine Ball Slam/Throw - 6 reps per side

5. Lower Body/Leg Power

Warm-Up (Back Squat):

  • ~30% 1RM x 5 reps
  • ~60-65% 1RM x 3 reps
  • ~75-80% 1RM x 2 reps

E1: Barbell Back Squat - 3-5 reps @ 80-85% 1RM

E2: Box Jumps or Depth Jumps - 5-8 reps (focus on height and power)

6. Boxing-Specific Power

F1: Landmine Jab (Heavy) - 6 reps per side (focus on explosive extension and rotational power)

F2: Plyometric Uppercut Throws with Medicine Ball - 6 reps per side
