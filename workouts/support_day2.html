Support — Push + Lower

Support Session (Low fatigue, complements CCT Day 2)

Session Summary

  • Type: Hypertrophy / Support
  • Focus: Shoulders + Triceps + Posterior Chain
  • Target Duration: ~40–55 minutes
  • Frequency: 1x/week (after CCT Day 2)
  • Goal: Build muscle without interfering with CCT performance

1. Shoulder Focus

A1: Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press - 3x8–12

Rest: ~60–90 seconds, then perform A2.

A2: Dumbbell Lateral Raise - 3x12–15

Rest: ~60–90 seconds, then repeat.

Rest / Setup: ~2–3 minutes

2. Triceps Focus

B1: Overhead Cable Triceps Extension - 3x10–12

Rest: ~60–90 seconds, then perform B2.

B2: Triceps Pushdown - 3x10–12

Rest: ~60–90 seconds, then repeat.

Rest / Setup: ~2–3 minutes

3. Posterior Chain

C1: Seated or Lying Leg Curl - 3x12–15

Rest: ~60–90 seconds, then perform C2.

C2: Romanian Deadlift (light DB or light barbell) - 2–3x8–12

Rest: ~60–90 seconds, then repeat.

Rest / Setup: ~2–3 minutes

4. Calves + Core

D1: Seated Calf Raise - 2–3x15–20

Rest: ~60–90 seconds, then perform D2.

D2: Hanging Leg Raises or Plank - 2–3 sets

Rest: ~60–90 seconds, then repeat.
