CCT Program 1

4 Workouts / 7 Days (Power + Support Split)

Overview

  • Structure: 2 CCT days + 2 support days
  • Goal: Maximize power output while building muscle
  • Recovery: 3 rest days per week
  • Cycle: Repeat weekly

Weekly Layout

  • Day 1: CCT Day 1 (Shoulders / Rotation / Boxing)
  • Day 2: Support 1
  • Day 3: Rest
  • Day 4: CCT Day 2 (Chest / Back / Legs)
  • Day 5: Support 2
  • Day 6: Rest
  • Day 7: Rest

Notes

  • Support sessions are designed to be low fatigue and should not impact CCT performance.
  • Prioritize quality and explosiveness on CCT days.
  • If recovery is limited, skip Day 5 and run a 3-day version.
  • Repeat this schedule weekly.
