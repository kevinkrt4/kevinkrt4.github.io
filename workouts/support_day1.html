Upper Support — Pull Emphasis

Support Session (Low fatigue, complements CCT Day 1)

Session Summary

  • Type: Hypertrophy / Support
  • Focus: Back + Biceps (minimal shoulder fatigue)
  • Target Duration: ~35–50 minutes
  • Frequency: 1x/week (performed after CCT Day 1)
  • Goal: Build muscle without interfering with CCT performance

1. Row Focus (Primary)

A1: Chest-Supported Row - 3x8–12

Rest: ~60–90 seconds, then perform A2.

A2: Lat Pulldown (neutral or supinated grip) - 3x8–12

Rest: ~60–90 seconds, then repeat.

Rest / Setup: ~2–3 minutes

2. Biceps Focus (Primary)

B1: Incline Dumbbell Curl - 3x10–12 (3 sec eccentric)

Rest: ~60–90 seconds, then perform B2.

B2: Hammer Curl (DB or rope) - 3x10–12

Rest: ~60–90 seconds, then repeat.

Rest / Setup: ~2–3 minutes

3. Light Press (Optional / Shoulder-Friendly)

C1: Incline Dumbbell Press (neutral grip) - 2x8–10

Rest: ~60–90 seconds, then repeat.

Rest / Setup: ~2–3 minutes

4. Arms Finish

D1: Cable Curl or EZ-Bar Curl - 2x10–12

Rest: ~60–90 seconds, then perform D2.

D2: Triceps Pushdowns - 2x10–12

Rest: ~60–90 seconds, then repeat.
