The Setup

A talking camel and a thirsty platypus are walking down a desert highway. A driver slows and asks what they’re doing out there.

The camel answers,
“We’re looking for the setup to a joke.”

The driver laughs and drives off.

They keep walking.

After a while, the platypus says,
“He laughed.”

The camel nods.
“Yeah.”

They walk a little further.

“We’re the only ones looking,” the platypus says.

The camel glances over.
“For the setup?”

The platypus nods.

“And he didn’t need it.”

The camel is quiet.

“Do you think…” it begins, then stops.

The platypus looks out at the road.

“I think it already happened.”

The camel takes a few more steps.

“The joke?” it asks.

The platypus nods once.

They keep moving down the highway, the desert stretching out in all directions.

Nothing missing.
Nothing to find.
